Packed with all the right tools

  • Role-based permissions system for single publishers or team collaboration
  • Light and dark modes that offer uniquely pleasant color palettes
  • A writing summary weekly digest that provides updates on trending content
  • Customizable routing options to keep your data secure in a production environment
  • Localized content that provides support for over 14 different languages
  • An autosaving editor that makes sure you never lose your place, or your sanity
  • A simple organization system of tags and topics helps you quickly find and maintain content
  • Advanced content scheduling that publishes your work exactly when you want it to

Publication statistics

Measure the impact of your posts with realtime data. Not everyone needs the headache of setting up Google Analytics, so we built in just the best parts, and added a couple new ones.

Beautiful editing experience

Enjoy a distraction-free writing experience in this Medium-inspired editor. More than just a formatting toolbar, you'll find out it has some tricks up its sleeve.

Unsplash integration

Canvas integrates the Unsplash API directly inside of the editor, allowing beautiful imagery to be easily inserted inside of impactful writing.

Search engine optimization

Publish once, distribute everywhere. Attach meta tags, descriptions, and canonical links to allow your content up to be discovered in as many ways as possible.
